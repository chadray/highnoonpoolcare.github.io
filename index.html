About High Noon Pool Care

At High Noon Pool Care, we keep your pool crystal clear and ready to enjoy all season long. Whether you need weekly maintenance, one-time cleaning, or green pool recovery, we handle it all with professionalism and care. We've earned the trust of North Texas homeowners by delivering reliable service, clear communication, and honest pricing.

Your pool should be a place of relaxation, not stress. Let us handle the chemicals, filter cleaning, and weekly inspections so you can spend time with family and friends instead of worrying about maintenance.

Our Services

🏊

Weekly Pool Service

Regular maintenance visits to keep your pool clean and balanced. We handle water testing, chemical adjustments, and equipment checks every week.

🧹

Pool Cleaning

Deep cleaning of your pool including brushing, vacuuming, and skimming debris. Your pool will be sparkling clean.

⚗️

Chemical Balancing

Precise water testing and chemical adjustments to maintain the perfect pH and chlorine levels for safe, clear water.

🔧

Filter Cleaning

Regular filter maintenance keeps your circulation system running efficiently. We clean or replace filters as needed.

💚

Green-to-Clean Service

Got a green or cloudy pool? We specialize in pool recovery. We'll bring your water back to safe, clear condition.

✓

One-Time Service Visits

Need a one-off cleaning or maintenance check? No problem. We offer flexible service for seasonal or occasional needs.
